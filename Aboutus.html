ABOUT TWENTY TWO

Twenty Two Above started out as a small buisness and grew larger and larger in the Gisborne reigon over the years. With an Amazing team of 5 ladies, Holly, Indy, Tara, Heather and owner and manager Leigh and larry, the store thrives in the heart of the city. Our team prides themselves with helping customers find excatly what they need in the fashion world. Our broad spectrum of womens and mens High fashion, street wear, swimwear, outer wear, and even infant clothing and a wide collection of footwear allows you to find something personal and perfect for you and your family. We specialise in bringing you a large diversity of clothing to provide you with a choice. Our stock list keeps on growing and we focus on providing a fit of clothing for everyone.

A locally owned buisness which thrives in the small town of Gisborne, New Zealand. The beach bound, sunny side, middle of no where town has some hidden gems and we are one of them. For years we have remained gisbornes biggest retailer locally owned and we certainly have the biggest range of stock. We hope that you enjoy your experience in our store and have a great time wearing our fancy gears!! Be sure to like us on facebook and instagram for your latest updates.
